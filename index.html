About Tracy

Therapy rooted in genuine connection

I'm Tracy Croghan, a Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker dedicated to helping adults work through life's most difficult challenges. Whether you're navigating anxiety, depression, grief, relationship struggles, or major life transitions — you don't have to face it alone.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and deeply individualized. I draw on evidence-based modalities including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based techniques, and trauma-informed care to meet you exactly where you are.

I believe therapy should feel like a genuine partnership — a place where you feel truly heard, respected, and empowered to build the life you want.
